CA (Dr.) Raj Chawla

Founding Partner, RCA and Co LLP

"Combining deep technical specialization with a listening-first approach to shape professional advice that is robust, commercially relevant, and built on trust."

CA (Dr.) Raj Chawla is the Founding Partner of RCA and Co LLP and a widely respected voice in the Indian Chartered Accountancy profession. A practicing Chartered Accountant since 1992, he brings over three decades of experience across audit and assurance, direct and indirect taxation, regulatory matters, transaction advisory and risk advisory for national and international clients.

His thought leadership and academic credentials — spanning law, company secretaryship, cost accountancy, and a doctorate — inform the firm's commitment to technically rigorous, commercially relevant advice. He plays an active role in driving professional education and mentoring the next generation of financial leaders.
